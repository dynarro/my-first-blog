What doesn't kill makes you stronger

It’s important not to doubt of yourself, to try learning from rejections and improve yourself. It’s also important, in my opinion, not to see everything that may be going bad at the moment and, instead, remember the things that prove you’re following the right path, the goals you have achieved until now, and specially if you’re a beginner, that rejections come with the package, and it’s just a matter of time and learning.
